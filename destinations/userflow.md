# Userflow

## 🏃‍♀️ Getting Started

1. Navigate to the **Destinations** page in Census and click **New Destination**.
2. Select **Userflow** from the menu.
3. Enter your **API Key** (found in the Userflow app) and select your **Userflow Plan**.
